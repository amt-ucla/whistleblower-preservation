Notable Whistleblowers

The term whistleblower is often applied to people who leak classified records, but as mentioned above, not all leaks are protected by law

Notable Whistleblowers

  • Daniel Ellsberg
  • W. Mark Felt
  • Chelsea Manning
  • Edward Snowden
  • Reality Winner

Daniel Ellsberg

Ellsberg photocopied and leaked the Pentagon Papers in 1969, which helped prove that the Johnson Administartion had lied to Congress

Where Archivists Might Encounter Leaked Documents

The examples above point to several common institutions that hold leaked documents, namely:

  • Government archives
  • Presidential or state libraries
  • News media archives
  • Non-profit or non-governmental organizations
  • Corporate archives
  • Academic archives
